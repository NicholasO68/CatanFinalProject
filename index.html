Settlers of Catan

Welcome to the game! Settlers of Catan is a game where up to 4 players compete to gather resources and build settlements on an island to earn up to 10 points to win!

Starting off, each player will be able to place one settlement and two roads in any lines of the board but must be two lines away from a player's existing spot.

The numbers on each hexagon will determine what number must be rolled on the dice to get the resources from that area.

But watch out for the robber! Whenever a seven is rolled, the robber is able to move and steal one piece of resources from the player and block anyone from getting resources

based on where the robber lands! Depending on what resources you have, you will be able to build either a road, a settlement, or a city. But roads and settlements/ctites must

be connected to gain points. That said, thank you for visiting our page and we hope you enjoy the game!
